HB 352 (As Passed the House) - 2026 Regular Session MISSISSIPPI LEGISLATURE 2026 Regular Session To: Rules By: Representative Creekmore IV House Bill 352 (As Passed the House) AN ACT TO DESIGNATE THE UNION COUNTY HERITAGE MUSEUM AS THE OFFICIAL STATE BARBEQUE MUSEUM; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI: SECTION 1.  The Union County Heritage Museum is designated as the official state barbeque museum. SECTION 2.  This act shall take effect and be in force from and after July 1, 2026.
